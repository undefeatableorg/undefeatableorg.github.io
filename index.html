THE FOUNDER

Elyse Robinson

Mexico City. One of the largest cities in the world. #espanol


After living in Mexico City, for almost two years and being immersed in the language and culture, I decided I want to give back. I came here on a hope and a dream after my mother passed away back in November 2016. I have been enjoying my life ever since. I wish I had done this much sooner along with learning a second language. Now that I know more about my new home, I want others to experience what I have, but at a much earlier age. And Mexico is the perfect place to do that! You can find out more about me and my story HERE. FIND OUT MORE ABOUT ME

ABOUT US

Learn about the organization and what we do

A nonprofit organization, Undefeatable Foundation changes the lives of American youth by helping them travel and learn a language. We help give students a valuable skill---another language. This is much needed in the 21st century and will make each student a valued global citizen.
